About Us

About us: We as students want to address a longstanding gap between private and public sector education. For this reason, we created a nonprofit NGO to raise funds needed for acquiring educational supplies, technology, and upgrading classrooms or even schools. We rely on long-term donations from large or small companies, other NGOs, individuals, or even students. Our target audience is children in remote areas of Huancayo.

Donations

Our Program seeks to improve the quality of education by providing up-to-date teaching materials, innovative technologies, and modern classrooms. Donations from companies, individuals, and NGOs will be used to purchase materials such as books and computers, build or renovate safe classrooms, train teachers in educational technologies, and cover operating costs. This will impact better academic results, motivation, and equitable access to education, in addition to developing digital skills and improving the quality of life of children. We will have regular reports, results monitoring, surveys, and supervisory visits to evaluate progress. We will maintain transparency through partnerships with local organizations, public reporting on the use of donations, and regular audits, ensuring that your support makes a tangible difference in these children's education.
